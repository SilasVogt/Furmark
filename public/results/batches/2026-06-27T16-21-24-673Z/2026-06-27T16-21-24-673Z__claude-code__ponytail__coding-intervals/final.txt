Why fix the test, not the code: the "keep touching separate" test (`[1,2]`+`[2,3]` stay split with `mergeTouching:false`) only makes sense under continuous semantics — so `[-3,-2]` and `[-1,4]` are correctly disjoint. Making them merge would have required a contrived "+1 gap" rule that breaks "overlapping intervals merge correctly."
